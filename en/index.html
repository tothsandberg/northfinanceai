Frequently asked

What executives typically want to know.

The four questions that come up most often in Strategy Calls — answered directly, without marketing language.

Three differences. First: we don't write reports. The deliverable is a working finance process — automated reconciliation, a 13-week cash forecast, a dunning workflow — running in your actual systems on the day we hand off.

Second: fixed price, not day rates. A consultancy is paid to take longer; we are paid to be faster.

Third: code-ownership. You get the scripts, the configuration, the documentation — and the right to walk away or hire someone else to maintain it. No retention play.

We work alongside them, not against them. The tax advisor remains responsible for legally reserved tasks: annual financial statements, tax returns, VAT submissions to the Finanzamt, audit support and tax advisory.

We work upstream: we take over the operational and bookkeeping tasks that are not reserved to tax advisors — ongoing finance operations, cash-flow planning, receivables management, controlling, and clean data preparation for the formal handover.

In practice the tax advisor receives cleaner data, has fewer corrections to make, and can focus on their core expertise. We proactively reach out to your tax advisor before project start to align on the chart of accounts, posting logic and data handover format.

Your financial data does not leave your infrastructure — the automations run locally in your environment, in your DATEV system or in a separate Python instance that only you control. When we use AI analysis (e.g. pattern recognition in receivables), we transmit to Anthropic — the US provider behind Claude — only technically pseudonymized data: numeric customer IDs, amounts, due dates. Never plain-text names, addresses, account details, or tax IDs.

Contractually secured by Data Processing Agreement (DPA) under GDPR Art. 28 and Standard Contractual Clauses under Art. 46 GDPR for US data transfer. If you prefer to exclude AI entirely, that's possible — we build the automation without an AI component. Slightly more manual configuration effort, comparable end result.

You do. After project delivery, you receive:
